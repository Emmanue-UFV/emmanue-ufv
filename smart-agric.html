Smart farming is a new idea that refers to the management of farms utilising technology like as IoT, robots, drones, and AI to raise the number and quality of goods while optimising the amount of human labour necessary for production.

Here at ISS, our smart farming platform is still in development. However, below are some of the groups we intend partnering with to fully integrate smart technology in Nigerian agricultural sector.

  • Farm21
  • Zenvus
  • Integrated Aerial Precision

Mission

Our mission is to serve as a technology-driven platform where Nigerians, regardless of gender, economic class, education, or any other socio-economic class, can interact, network, and exchange information, ideas, and resources pertinent to poverty alleviation and rural developemt, via sustainable agricuture.

Vision

To have lifted 50 million Nigerians out of extreme poverty by 2030.
