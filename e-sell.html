Mission

Our mission is to serve as a technology-driven platform where Nigerians, regardless of gender, economic class, education, or any other socio-economic class, can interact, network, and exchange information, ideas, and resources pertinent to poverty alleviation and rural developemt, via sustainable agricuture.
