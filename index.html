Integrated Smart Solution Platform (ISSP)

Concept of ISSP

ISSP is a an technology-driven platform where Nigerians, regardless of gender, economic class, education, or any other socio-economic class, can interact, network, and exchange information, ideas, and resources pertinent to poverty alleviation and rural developemt, via sustainable agricuture.

Mission

Our mission to serve as a an technology-driven platform where Nigerians, regardless of gender, economic class, education, or any other socio-economic class, can interact, network, and exchange information, ideas, and resources pertinent to poverty alleviation and rural developemt, via sustainable agricuture.

Vision

To have lifted 200 million Nigerians out of extreme poverty by 2020.

Participants

You are especially invited to join us if you:

  1. Own a farm (be it plant or animal) or are interested in starting a farm
  2. Are a firm, governmental or non-govenrmental, willing to work with Nigerian farmers and:
    • Provide loans for farms
    • Lease agricultural equipments
    • Provide logistics services
    • Provide any other agro-relavant services, such as logistics, food processing, or precision technology for farms
  3. Are individuals or companies interested in purchasing agro-products
  4. Are individuals in providing agro-related services, such as e-commerce, educational resources, etc.

What do you stand to gain

We invite you to be the forerunners of smart agriculture in Nigeria. The opportunities you stand to gain, as individuals or firms, are immeasurable. Imagine yourself with the whole of stakeholders of Agribusiness in Nigeria, in a conference room!!
